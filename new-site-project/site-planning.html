Overview

Purpose

Kindle is best when you are looking for a specific book that you are wanting to own or spend money on, but if you are looking to ‘borrow books’ and not spend as much money then Kindle Unlimited is the better choice. Kindle Unlimited is a subscription that you can get through Kindle that, for a monthly fee, you can borrow up to 10 books. You can keep them as long as you would like and change them out whenever you want. Any book that kindle has that is Kindle Unlimited is available for you to borrow to your hearts content.

Audience

The target audience can be huge for Kindle, Kindle Unlimited, especially all the lover of good books. Student, lecturer or researcher looking for gaining knowledge through books. Have you ever wanted to read or listen to a good book but don’t know how to whittle down the search to what you are actually looking for? Finding the right book with the right story line, outcome, page limit, word choice, etc. can be exhausting and time consuming. Then there is finding the right platform to look through. Kindle, Kindle Unlimited, Audible, OverDrive, Libby, library, and so many more. With so many different options and categories this can look like a daunting task. Don’t give up! With reading at least 1 to 2 books a day I have found some helpful tips and tricks that make finding the right book quick and easy! From finding quick reads to 400+ pages, fantasy to non-fiction, these tips and tricks will make you a wiz at finding what you need quickly and efficiently.
