What options are out there

First step is knowing what options are out there. Kindle have millions if not billions of books. Kindle unlimited is a subscription within Kindle. Audible is for audio books, podcasts, reviews, and more. OverDrive is an online library portal. Basically if your library has an online version, and you have a library card, you can rent out books, movies, audio books basically whatever your library has to offer. Libby is a subcategory of OverDrive. Then there are ongoing book sites where books can be posted by chapters rather than as a whole. However, most of those sites cost money to read more than 3-5 chapters a day.

Books are new universes, world, timelines, galaxies, and more that you have yet to explore!
