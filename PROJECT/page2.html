Finding the right book!

Kindle & Kindle Unlimited

Kindle is best when you are looking for a specific book that you are wanting to own or spend money on, but if you are looking to 'borrow books' and not spend as much money then Kindle Unlimited is the better choice. Kindle Unlimited is a subscription that you can get through Kindle that, for a monthly fee, you can borrow up to 10 books. You can keep them as long as you would like and change them out whenever you want. Any book that kindle has that is Kindle Unlimited is available for you to borrow to your hearts content.

OverDrive

OverDrive is best for both audio and reading books as long as you have a good online library and can be patient. With having to share the copies of books available in the library sometimes wait lists can be as long as 8 to 12 weeks. Having the option for both in the same place is nice, but can sometimes be a little hard to actually get the book.

Audible

Audible is best for audio books! Audible may only offers audio files, but with their vast collection from books, to podcasts, to reviews and more you will never run out of things to listen to. However, like Kindle you will have to pay for the books you want to listen too unless you sign up for one of their programs like Plus Catalog. Plus Catalog is just like Kindle Unlimited but just for audio books.
